MEDICINE

Chatbots can also help in synergizing front office healthcare; patients can pre-emptively provide information to the bot. This information that their bed-side nurses or doctors will use to organize post discharge follow-ups. Bots can streamline admissions, discharge, and transfer requests, schedule patient consultation requests and send and receive referrals. And while it may seem impossible to integrate information sources, chatbots can create a single system of records by transferring data from legacy systems to new databases, saving the systems time and money. Chatbots can save thousands of working hours a year for hospitals and doctors, taking the burden of doctors and making hospitals easier for customers.

BANKING

A chatbot is available 24/7 and with a 99% uptime, so there’s no need to only work bankers hours anymore. Chatbots can be trained to serve any purpose you deem for it. Customers can have an on-demand personal banker, wealth manager, loan service agent or an ATM locator at their fingertips. Chatbots make banking as easy as sending a text, instantly responding to queries from mundane requests to complicated tasks.

CHATBOT

the last few years enterprises have been suffering interruptions due to the new technologies or even to the consolidation of existing technologies. The communication between enterprises and their clients or contact center, has been affected by messaging applications. Due to the frustration provoked by the negative experiences of the traditional telephonic contacts, with poorly designed IVR systems, and the lack of services for the effective resolution of problems, the messaging applications appears as a way to solve this communication problem between enterprises and clients, reducing costs and bringing a better experience for the client. The real challenge for the enterprises is to offer a communication experience in these messages channels to the clients, in the same way that they use them to communicate to their friends and family. The channel will stay asynchronically open for the client to communicate with the enterprise when it’s needed.
Communication experience between the enterprise and the client is what Jumbo Enterprises solutions offers and that’s the purpose of this proposal to any company. First we will define what a bot is, its advantages and disadvantages and then we will describe the Agents and Supervisor training service where, through the AgentBot conversation driver, guides the session to a live chat, only when needed, offering a transparent Bot-Human-Bot experience. The agent will receive the chat history and the client profile for a customized attention. Once the agent finishes, the session returns to the bot to continue with the service. The conversational bots are programs that simulate a conversation with a person by providing automatic answers to the questions made by users. By using Artificial Intelligence technology, the bots can learn with the pass of time, and by that, maintain each time more natural conversations to the eyes of the interlocutor.
