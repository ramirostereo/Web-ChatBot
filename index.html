CHATBOT


The last few years enterprises have been suffering interruptions due to the new technologies or even to the consolidation of existing technologies. The communication between enterprises and their clients or contact center, has been affected by messaging applications. Due to the frustration provoked by the negative experiences of the traditional telephonic contacts, with poorly designed IVR systems, and the lack of services for the effective resolution of problems, the messaging applications appears as a way to solve this communication problem between enterprises and clients, reducing costs and bringing a better experience for the client.

The real challenge for the enterprises is to offer a communication experience in these messages channels to the clients, in the same way that they use them to communicate to their friends and family. The channel will stay asynchronically open for the client to communicate with the enterprise when it’s needed.

Communication experience between the enterprise and the client is what Jumbo Enterprises solutions offers.
First we define what a bot is, its advantages and disadvantages and then together we determine the Agents and Supervisor training service where, through the AgentBot conversation driver, guides the session to a live chat, only when needed, offering a transparent Bot-Human-Bot experience. The agent receives the chat history and the client profile for a customized attention. Once the agent finishes, the session returns to the bot to continue with the service. The conversational bots are programs that simulate a conversation with a person by providing automatic answers to the questions made by users. By using Artificial Intelligence technology, the bots can learn through time, making conversation more natural and human every time.

  • Available 24/7

    • The chatbots must be efficient to allow the user to solve a problem in the minimum possible time. To grant that, the virtual assistant must be available when the user needs it with no restrictions of time, and of course, with no operative load. All by a simple and friendly interface.

  • Keep the context

    • Humans do this constantly during conversations, and by that, they assume conversation systems that can adapt to that situation. The context can be based on aspects like previous conversations, the location, external events or even social states. This context is not necessarily obtained by the bot`s own initiative, it may also be immersed in the dialog flow. That's to say, the chatbot asks in order to obtain information and then act in consequence.

  • Offer a customized experience

    • When we talk about chatbots or conversational bots, we have to consider that it’s a software, and therefore, the customization can be limited. In all cases, it’s important that the conversation between the bot and the user to be the most customized and fluid as possible.

MEDICINE
Chatbots can also help in synergizing front office healthcare; patients can pre-emptively provide information to the bot. This information that their bed-side nurses or doctors will use to organize post discharge follow-ups. Bots can streamline admissions, discharge, and transfer requests, schedule patient consultation requests and send and receive referrals. And while it may seem impossible to integrate information sources, chatbots can create a single system of records by transferring data from legacy systems to new databases, saving the systems time and money. Chatbots can save thousands of working hours a year for hospitals and doctors, taking the burden of doctors and making hospitals easier for customers.
BANKING
With a banking chatbot, financial institutions can be there whenever their customers need them, regardless of time or location. A chatbot is available 24/7 and with a 99% uptime, so there’s no need to only work bankers hours anymore. Chatbots can be trained to serve any purpose you deem for it. Customers can have an on-demand personal banker, wealth manager, loan service agent or an ATM locator at their fingertips. Chatbots make banking as easy as sending a text, instantly responding to queries from mundane requests to complicated tasks.
